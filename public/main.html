Tyler Nygren is a chiropractic student living in Marietta, Georgia. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Biology in 2019 and is on-track to complete his Doctor of Chiropractic in March of 2022. He is currently working as an intern at the Life University Center for Health and Optimum Performance. In October of 2021, he will begin his six-month preceptorship with Dr. Bob Bacon, DC, at East Cobb Spine and Sport Chiropractic. Upon graduation, Tyler will be a third generation chiropractor. He is preceeded by his mother, Dr. Nichole Nygren, DC, and his late grandfather, Dr. Terry Rainey, DC. He will join the family practice, Nygren Chiropractic and Acupuncture, in the Spring of 2022.

Originally from Ohio, Tyler is a 2017 graduate of Van Wert High School. He was an active member of the cross country team, theatre department, and robotics club. In his childhood he developed an affinity for sleight-of-hand, and performed as a magician at local events. He now spends much of his free time developing for the web.
