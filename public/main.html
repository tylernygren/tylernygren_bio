Hi there!

I am a third generation chiropractic student, on-pace to complete the Doctor of Chiropractic degree program in March. I currently work as an intern at the Life University Center for Health and Optimum Performance, and, in May, I will begin work at Nygren Chiropractic and Acupuncture in Van Wert, Ohio.

I live twenty miles northwest of downtown Atlanta-- and I love it. I spend much of my free time finding new coffee shops and working on projects that interest me. In the last year, I have taken a particular interest in web development. I built this website, actually!
